Silver Moon Brewing crafts all of our beer out of Bend, Oregon. Thanks for letting us be your favorite local Bend brewery. Our brewers, Jeff Schauland and Alex Sarames, bring an eclectic style to our signature beers. We appreciate all of our local brewers not only at Silver Moon, but all over Bend. This one great challenge unites us all: satisfying our thirst for a well-crafted beer in Bend, and contribute great brews to our favorite communities in Central Oregon.
